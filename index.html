Yorgos Pantis

PhD Candidate in Machine Learning

I am a PhD candidate in Machine Learning at the National and Kapodistrian University of Athens. My research interests include optimization, statistical learning theory, and the mathematics of deep learning.

I obtained my MSc in Statistical Science from the University of Oxford and my BSc in Mathematics from the University of Patras.
