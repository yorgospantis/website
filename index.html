I am a first-year Ph.D. student at the National and Kapodistrian University of Athens and Archimedes, Athena Research Centre, working on theory-driven Machine Learning models, including Transformers and Neural Networks. I am supervised by Christos Tzamos.

Previously, I conducted research at DTU, CTU, Athena Research Center, and Max Planck Institute, focusing on Machine Learning, Optimization, and Natural Language Processing. My statistical and mathematical background comes from master's and bachelor's studies at the University of Oxford and University of Patras.

I grew up in the village of Agioi Pantes on Zakynthos, an island in Greece with rich history and natural beauty.
